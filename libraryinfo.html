About Our Library

The SK Kampung Sawah Library is a cheerful and student-friendly environment dedicated to promoting reading habits and providing access to valuable learning resources. The library supports academic learning and nurtures curiosity among students through various reading programs and engaging activities.

Vision

To foster a lifelong love for reading and learning in every student.

Mission & Objectives

Objective Description
Diverse Reading Materials Provide materials suited for all reading levels and interests.
Interactive Environment Offer fun and engaging setups that promote learning through play and participation.
Support Learning Equip students with knowledge and skills for academic success through curated content.

Facilities

Facility Purpose
Reading Corner Quiet and comfortable space for personal reading time.
Storytelling Area Interactive space for storytelling sessions and book readings.
Multimedia Resources Access to digital learning tools and materials.
Comfortable Seating Student-friendly furniture to enhance learning comfort.
